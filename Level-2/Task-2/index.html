Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

About

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a key role in India’s missile and nuclear programs and inspired millions of students.

Why I Admire Him

He believed in dreaming big, hard work, and serving the nation with humility and dedication.

Famous Quote

“Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action.”
